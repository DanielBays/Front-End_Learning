The New Year

Published on January 01, 2024

    With the new year, I have some New Year's resolutions of my own. These resolutions are going to be focused around my professional growth as a coder and my knowledge with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I want to code every weekday for at least an hour. I want to have three projects to add to my portfolio. I want to design a professionally designed portfolio by the beginning of June. I think it's important to keep goals achievable, and I would love to say I will start my new career as a Front-End Developer but with the research I've done, I think that will be more likely to occur in 2025 when I am able to network with more developers. I am still very new to all of this, and my goals may morph depending on new knowledge and the experience I gain from this journey. I am still working on setting up socials for this journey to help you all follow along if you would like. I would also love any feedback. and constructive criticism. I want to improve, and I know I will mess up (a lot) along the way. Please be patient with me and help. keep me accountable!

Best Regards and Happy New Years!
Daniel Bays
